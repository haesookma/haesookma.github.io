International Inbound & Outbound Tourists and Expenditures

By: Haesook Ma
May 8, 2021

International Tourists hitting the peak up until the outbreak of COVID-19

The number of internation tourists has increased every year until 2019. World Bank provides the inbound and outbound tourists by country from 1995 to 2019. Currently, the data for 2020 is not available yet, but it would be an outlier data due to the outbreak of COVID-19. So, in this article, we will analyze the international inbound and outbound tourism trend from 1995 to 2019.

Top 30 Countries - Number of International Tourists Arrival in 2019

The following chart shows the Top 30 countries based on the number of international tourists arrival in 2019. As you can see here, the United States is the number one international tourism destination country, and China and Spain are the second and third countries.

This chart also has a color feature showing the number of international tourists departure from each country. The United States and China have the highest number of tourists arrival and departure at the same time. However, in case of Spain, although it has many tourists coming to Spain, it does not have many tourists going to overseas from Spain. In contrast, in case of Germnay, the color of the bar chart is darker than the countries next to it, and it shows that there are many German people going to overseas compared to the number of international tourists visiting Germany.

Additionally, the following bar chart shows the number of international tourists arrival and the color of each bar shows the international tourism expenditures. This chart shows that the United States is the number one country of international tourists arrival, and at the same time they spend the most in tourism.

The number of international tourists has gradually increased from 1995 to 2019. There has been a different trend between countries, but the overall trend is similar. Please check the number of international tourists arrival from 1995 to 2019 by clicking the name of country.
